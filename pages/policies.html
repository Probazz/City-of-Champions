School Policies

Our policies are designed to create a safe, respectful, and productive learning environment for all students.

Attendance Policy

Regular attendance is essential for academic success. Students are expected to attend school daily and arrive on time.

Dress Code

Students must wear the approved school uniform neatly and properly at all times within school premises.

Discipline

Respect, responsibility, and good behavior are expected from all students. Misconduct will be addressed appropriately.

Health & Safety

The school ensures a safe environment. Students should report any injuries or unsafe situations immediately.

Academic Integrity

Cheating, plagiarism, and dishonesty are not tolerated. Students are expected to maintain honesty in all academic work.

Use of Technology

Technology must be used responsibly. Unauthorized use of devices during class is prohibited.
